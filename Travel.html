Travel

      I have always loved travelling since I was young lad.

      My family and I go on holiday most summers.

      We usually go to France or Spain, but we have gone to England the past couple of years.

Spain

  1. My first memory of going on holidays was to Barcelona, Spain when I was 5 years old with my family.

  2. We mostly stayed in Barcelona and Salou when we went to Spain, but one year, we went to the Basque country.

  3. I have seen a couple of soccer games whilst there, primarily Espanyol FC games (as Barcelona FC games are very expensive).

  4. Another attraction in Barcelona/Salou is the Portaventura entertainment park, which we visited every time we went to the area.

  5. The Basque country was also very interesting to visit, as it very different culturally from the rest of Spain, much like Catalonia, where Barcelona is located.

  6. I managed to get to an Athletic Bilbao match while there, which was very entertaining.


France

  1. France was where my family and I went on holidays the most.

  2. We have been all around France, staying in a variety of caravan parks and camping sites.

  3. I enjoyed these places the most, as I was given a lot of freedom of where I could go, that I didn't have at home.

  4. We also visited Paris, and Disneyland, a couple of times.

  5. I enjoyed visiting French historical sites and museums immensely, especially Medieval sites, due to my interest in history.


England

  1. In the last few years, my family and I have started going to England for our holidays.

  2. We usually go for city breaks, in cities such as Liverpool, Manchester and London.

  3. We have also visited theme parks while in England, such as Alton Towers, near Stoke.

  4. My brother and I also go to Premier League soccer games every year.

  5. Since we support Spurs and Chelsea respectively, we try to get tickets for this match, though since it is a derby game, this isn't always possible.
