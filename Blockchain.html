Blockchain

  1. A blockchain, is a growing list of records, called blocks, which are linked using cryptography.

  2. Due to its design, blockchain is resistant to retroactive of its records, as any changes made must be ratified by others in the peer-to-peer network.

  3. This openness combined with security makes it well suited for cryptocurrency,as it does not need a central authority to regulate it.

  4. Examples of cryptocurrency are Bitcoin, Litecoin, and Ethereum.

  5. Blockchain is also being implemented in the Financial Services industry, such as distributed ledgers for banking use.

  6. Walmart and IBM are partnering to implement blockchain to track and monitor Walmart's supply chain.
